Phase 3 Documentation

Include The 2 initial scouting locations, with artist statements on each place and their signifigance.

Alum Rock Mountain

Alum Rock Mountain is a challenging and rewarding hike that offers some of the most breathtaking views of the San Jose area. A phrase to describe the sunrise hike at Alum Rock Mountain could be "Valley's Most Ethereal Sunrise View" as it offers hikers a chance to witness a sunrise over the valley that is both breathtakingly beautiful and almost otherworldly.

Lake Elizabeth

Located in the Fremont, Central Park, offers gorgeous views of the Mission Peak Hills and Lake Elizabeth. Central Park is set on 450+ acres with an 80+ acre lake and 2 mile walkway around the lake. Lake Elizabeth is one of the most stunning man-made wonders in the region, with its crystal-clear waters, breathtaking mountain views, and serene surroundings that will leave you in awe.
