The Partnership Project

Backstory

This piece was my graduation gift to celebrate my friend Lulu's successful completion of her master's degree. During the early stages of the design, Lulu and I worked together to complete the sketches and content of the piece. However, she had to return home early for personal reasons and was unable to participate in the subsequent work. Nevertheless, we continued to communicate and collaborate, and eventually decided to create a Fridge magnet as a graduation gift for her through 3D printing.
